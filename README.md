# ⏳ TECHFEST CHRONOS  
## The Timeline of Tomorrow 🚀

An immersive futuristic **interactive parallax scrolling experience** created for **Techfest IIT Bombay**.

TECHFEST CHRONOS transforms a traditional webpage into a cinematic journey where users travel across different eras of innovation:

**Past → Present → Future**

Built with futuristic visuals, smooth animations, interactive elements, and storytelling-based scrolling.

## ✨ Concept

CHRONOS is designed as a digital time machine that explores the evolution of technology and innovation.

The experience takes users through:

### 🌌 Origin Era
The beginning of technological evolution and innovation culture.

### ⚡ Present Era
Celebrating modern breakthroughs, AI, robotics, startups, and the spirit of Techfest.

### 🔮 Future Era
A vision of 2050 powered by artificial intelligence, space technology, and human-machine collaboration.
